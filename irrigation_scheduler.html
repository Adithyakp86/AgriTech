Smart Irrigation Tips

Best Time to Water

Water early morning (4-8 AM) to minimize evaporation and fungal growth.

Temperature Awareness

Increase watering during heatwaves and reduce during cool, humid periods.

Rain Check

Skip irrigation if rain is expected within 24 hours to save water.

Crop Stage

Seedling stage needs frequent light watering, mature crops need deep watering.
